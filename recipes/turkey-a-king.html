Turkey Ala King

This recipe is a family favorite after Thanksgiving or anytime there is leftover turkey. I often freeze single serving for a simple meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Chopped Turkey
  • 4oz Mushrooms (fresh or canned)
  • 1 Small Green Pepper
  • 1 Can Peas and Carrots (or 1 1/2 cups frozen), also can use leftover Green Beans
  • 2 Tblsp Flour
  • 4 Tblsp Butter
  • 1 to 1 1/2 Cups Milk
  • 1 to 1 1/2 Cups Turkey Gravy
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In medium sauce pan, saute mushrooms and peppers in 2 tablespoons of butter for approx 5 min, remove from heat and set aside
  2. In same pan, blend 2 Tbsps butter with 2 tbsps flour, add some salt and papper, slowely add 1 cup milk, stirring constantly to keep the mixture a consistancy of gravy
  3. Add turkey, gravy, peppers, mushrooms, and veggies
  4. Heat to boiling, reduce heat adding milk if needed to obtain the desired consistancy
  5. Again, add salt and pepper to taste
  6. serve over toast, rice or flat noodles
  7. Freeze single servings for easy week night meals
