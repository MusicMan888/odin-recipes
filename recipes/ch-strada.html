Mom's Cheese Strada

This recipe is from the family cook book, more like a family tradition vs family recipe, Something my Mom made for Christmas morning that could be popped in the oven, and served to everyone, Champagne optional for the adults, perhaps a non alcoholic version for the kids. It got everone in the spirit for opening gifts, and then a nice Christmas nap.

Ingredients - tbd

  • 1 Lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 1 Can Tomato Soup
  • 3 Cans Light or Dark Red Kidney Beans or Chili Beans (or one of each)
  • 1 Small Can of Crushed Tomatos
  • 2 Tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 Tsp White Vinegar
  • 1/4 Tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground Red Pepper (add more red pepper for a hotter chili)

Steps

  1. In a large casserole pan (5 Qt) add ground beef and chopped onion
  2. Heat on medium high heat until ground beef is fully cooked
  3. Break up ground beef into smaller 1/2 inch pieces
  4. Drain grease from cooked onions and ground beef
  5. Add all remaining ingredients to pan and increase heat until contents boil, stirring occasionally
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
