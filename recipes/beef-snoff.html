Classic Beef Stroganoff

This recipe is from the family cook book, but not likely a family recipe. Just a nice classic recipe to enjoy over rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lb Sirloin or Tenderloin
  • 1/2 Lb Fresh Mushrooms sliced
  • 1/2 Cup minced Onion
  • 3 Tbsp Flour
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 Can (10 1/2 OZ) Beef Consomme
  • 2 Tsp Ketchup
  • 1 small minced Garlic clove
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Sour Cream (8 OZ)
  • 3 - 4 Cups Hot Cooked Noodles or Rice

Steps

  1. Cut meat diagonally into very thin slices
  2. In medium skillet, saute onions and mushrooms in butter until onions are tender, remove and set aside
  3. In same skillet, brown meat on both sides
  4. Set aside 1/3 cup of beef Consomme
  5. Stir remaining consomee, ketchup, garlic, and salt into skillet, cover and simmer for 15 min.
  6. Blend reserved 1/3 cup of consomee and flour, stir into skillet
  7. Add mushroom and onion, heat to boiling, stirring constantly
  8. Boil 20 minutes, stir in sour cream and heat through
  9. Serve over hot noodles or rice
